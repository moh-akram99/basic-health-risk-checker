Basic Health Risk Checker

A simple command-line tool for quick health screening

What is this tool?

Calculate Your Risk

The Basic Health Risk Checker is a lightweight command-line application that helps users quickly assess basic health risk indicators using simple and transparent rules.

How does it work?

  • The user enters age, weight, and height
  • The tool calculates the Body Mass Index (BMI)
  • The BMI is classified into standard categories
  • A basic health risk level is displayed

Key Features

  • Fast and easy to use
  • Offline and privacy-friendly
  • No personal data storage
  • Clear and rule-based results

Who is it for?

  • Students and educators
  • Healthcare trainees
  • Anyone needing a quick health check

Why use this tool?

This tool provides a simple way to automate basic health calculations without relying on complex systems or online platforms. It is designed for educational and preliminary screening purposes.
